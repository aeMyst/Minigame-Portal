Board Setup:
Played on a vertical 6x7 grid.

Players:
Two players take turns dropping one of their colored discs into a column.

Objective:
Be the first to form a line of four discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Gameplay:
Players take turns dropping their disc into one of the columns.
Discs stack on top of each other in the chosen column.

Winning:
The first player to connect four discs wins.
If the board fills completely without a winner, the game ends in a draw.

Valid Moves:
A Tile can only be placed in a column that is not full.
