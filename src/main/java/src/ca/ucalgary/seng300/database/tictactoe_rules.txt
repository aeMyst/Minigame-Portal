Board Setup:
Played on a 3x3 grid.

Players:
Two players take turns: one as "X" and the other as "O".

Objective:
Be the first to get three of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Gameplay:
Players alternate turns, placing their symbol in an empty square.
Once a symbol is placed, it cannot be moved or removed.

Winning:
The first player to form a line of three wins.
If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
